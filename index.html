Zucchini Brownies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of zucchini
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder, unsweetened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp vanilla
  • Non-stick spray/Butter spray

optinal toppings

  • chocolate chips
  • Nuts
  • sprinkles

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. spray bottom of baking dish with butter or non-stick spray.
  3. Mix all of the designated Ingredients together.
  4. Transfer mixture into sprayed baking dish.
  5. Add optinal toppings if you would like.
  6. Bake 20-25 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
